About Me

I'm a Masters student at the National University of Singapore pursuing a Masters in Computer Science, having recently concluded my bachelors in Computer Engineering. My passion for the intersection of hardware and software systems has driven me to explore and contribute to this interdisciplinary field.

I have a particular interest in Deep Learning, especially deep reinforcement learning, reasoning, and post-training of language models. Currently, I'm working on research at the Scalable AI Lab under Prof. Lu Yao, focusing on post-training of LLMs to create specialized knowledge-aware agents.

My experience spans from building machine learning models for medical data analysis to developing full-stack applications and working with robotic systems. I'm always eager to gain valuable experience, continue learning, and contribute to the frontier in AI and related fields.
